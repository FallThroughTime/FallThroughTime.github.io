Fall Through Time




Spring comes and things start to grow.





Trees are full of leaves.





New trees start growing.





To become big in years to come.





Than fall come and the leaves start to change colours.





They change to beautiful colours.





Some trees change quicker than others.





But before you know it, leaves start to fall.





Leaves leave the tree naked.





Some leaves fall before changing.





The leaves end up not here, but...





...everywhere else!





Leaves everywhere make fun to play in.





Then the cold comes and the leaves wait for another year to come.
